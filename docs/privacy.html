Noctera

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Your dreams never leave your phone. Noctera has no servers, no account, and no way to see what you record.

What we collect

Nothing. Noctera collects, transmits, sells and shares no personal data. No sign-up, no login, no cloud.

Where your data lives

Your dreams, symbols, tags, readings and settings are stored locally on your iPhone. Voice is transcribed on-device using Apple's Speech framework; audio is not uploaded.

Dream interpretation

Readings are generated on your device — a built-in symbol lexicon, and on supported devices Apple's on-device foundation models. Your dreams are never sent to any server for analysis.

Notifications

Reminders are scheduled and delivered entirely on-device. No content goes to a server.

Purchases

Noctera Pro is a one-time purchase handled by Apple (StoreKit). We never see your payment details.

Analytics & tracking

None. No third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.
