Noctera helps you capture dreams the instant you wake, read their symbols, and see what recurs — all on your device.

How do I record a dream fast?

Add the Log a Dream control to your Control Center or Action Button, or tap the Lock Screen widget — Noctera opens straight into recording. Speak; it transcribes and saves itself. You can also type.

Does Noctera connect to the cloud?

No. Everything is on-device — no account, no server, no tracking. Your dreams stay yours.

What's in Noctera Pro?

A one-time $4.99 unlock (no subscription): full readings, personalized on-device AI, pattern insights, shareable cards, lucid reality-check reminders, and export/backup.

I bought Pro on another device

Open Settings → Restore Purchases. Pro is tied to your Apple ID.

How do I move my dreams to a new phone?

Settings → Back up (all data) saves a file; on the new phone use Restore from backup. Your data is portable and yours.
